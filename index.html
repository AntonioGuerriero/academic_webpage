Antonio Guerriero

Ph.D.

University of Naples Federico II

Biography

Ph.D. in Information Technologies and Electrical Engineering (ITEE) and member of Dependable and Secure Software Engineering and Real-Time Systems (DESSERT) group at the University of Naples Federico II. His research interests include software testing, software reliability, and testing of autonomous systems.
